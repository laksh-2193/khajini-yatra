Khajni Yatra: A Journey of Listening and Understanding Rural India

Khajni Yatra is not just a political initiative, but a sincere effort to connect with people at the grassroots level. Through this journey, I will be visiting villages, staying among the people, and spending time with them to truly understand their daily lives, challenges, and aspirations.

Too often, policies and manifestos are created without fully understanding the ground realities. The purpose of Khajni Yatra is to bridge that gap, so that every decision and every point in the manifesto is shaped directly by the voices, experiences, and needs of the people.

This journey is not only about listening to problems but also about working towards practical and meaningful solutions. It is built on trust, dialogue, and participation, where every voice matters and is given importance.

Khajni Yatra is a commitment, to stand with the people, live among them, and ensure that their concerns are truly represented.
